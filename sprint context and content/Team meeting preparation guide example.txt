Introduction: Why This Sprint Matters

This sprint is crucial for improving your team's dynamics and overall performance. By identifying and addressing dysfunctions, your team can become more cohesive, effective, and aligned toward achieving collective goals. Engaging fully in this sprint will empower you to tackle existing challenges, streamline teamwork, and enhance the overall effectiveness of your group.

By the end of this sprint, your team will:
* Gain a clear understanding of the key dysfunctions affecting your team dynamics.
* Develop actionable strategies to overcome these dysfunctions and improve collaboration.
* Be better equipped to foster an environment of trust, accountability, and commitment.

If the team does not fully engage in this sprint:
* Dysfunctional behaviors may persist, hindering the team's performance and morale.
* Misalignment within the team could lead to ineffective decision-making and poor execution of tasks.
* The team's overall cohesion and ability to achieve collective goals may be compromised.

Preparation Requirements
What to Prepare:
1. Individual Assessment:
o Complete the Individual Assessment Tool focusing on the 5 Dysfunctions of a Team. Reflect honestly on how these dysfunctions manifest in your team and be prepared to discuss your findings.
o Suggested Time: 30 minutes
2. Review of Last Sprint:
o Reflect on the key learnings and outcomes from the last sprint. Consider how these insights can be applied in the current context of team dynamics.
o Suggested Time: 15 minutes
3. Team Summary Preparation:
o Compile your individual assessments and be ready to contribute to the team summary discussion. This will help in identifying the most pressing dysfunctions that need collective attention.
o Suggested Time: 30 minutes

Agenda for the Team Sprint Meeting
Total Meeting Duration: 90 minutes
o Do Better Than Last Time - 10 minutes
o  Reflect on the last sprint's performance and discuss how the team can improve in this sprint.
o Topic Presentation by the Guru - 20 minutes
o  The Guru will present an overview of the 5 Dysfunctions of a Team, emphasizing their impact on team dynamics and performance.
o Discussion on Team Dysfunctions -  30 minutes
o Engage in an open discussion based on the compiled individual assessments. Identify the most critical dysfunctions and discuss how they affect the team's performance.
o Decision-Making and Planning  20 minutes
o  Develop a clear action plan to address the identified dysfunctions. Assign responsibilities and set deadlines for each action.
o Reflection Time 10 minutes
o Reflect on the meeting's outcomes and how the team will move forward with the agreed-upon actions.

Final Thoughts and Tips
Engagement:
Your active participation is crucial. Be honest in your assessments, open in discussions, and committed to the decisions made during the meeting.
Consistency:
Ensure that all contributions are aligned with the team's overall goals. Consistency in approach and follow-through will be key to the success of this sprint.
Support:
If you have any questions or need clarification on the tools or the process, don't hesitate to reach out to the Guru or facilitator. Your understanding and commitment are vital to the team's progress.
